The Current Video Games

The current videos games that are popular right now concise of Minecraft, Fortnite, League of Legends and Valorant. All of these games are fun and engaging, but another similarity they have is that they are all multiplayer. This allows for the replayablility of the each game is increased because you aren't playing by yourself and each game is different.

• Minecraft
- Minecraft is a game made up of blocks, creatures, and community. You can survive the night or build a work of art, the choice is all yours.
• Fortnite
- Fortnite is a battle royale game, developed by Epic Games, where players compete to be the last one standing. It is also a player-versus-environment cooperative game, where players work together to complete missions and survive.
• Valorant
- Valorant is a team-based first-person tactical hero shooter set in the near future. Players play as one of a set of Agents, characters based on several countries and cultures around the world. In the main game mode, players are assigned to either the attacking or defending team with each team having five players on it.
• League of Legends
- League of Legends is a multiplayer online battle arena (MOBA) game where players control a character with unique abilities from an isometric perspective.
