History of Video Games

The history of video games started between the 1950s and 1960s as computer scientist began creating simple games on minicomputers and mainframes. In 1962, Spacewar! was developed by MIT as one of the first such games on video display.The first video game hardware was released in the 1970s and the first home game to be released would be Magnavox Odyssy. The first arcade games would be Pong and Computer Space.
